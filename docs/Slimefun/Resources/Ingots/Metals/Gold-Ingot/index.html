Gold Ingot

Slimefun adds 11 new Gold Ingots to the game.

They are differentiated by their purity (measured in "carats"), with the lowest purity being 4 and the highest being 24.

取得​

Smelting Gold Dust in a Smeltery or an Electric Ingot Factory produces a 4-carat Gold Ingot.

Higher purity gold is obtained by smelting any carat gold along with a piece of Gold Dust. This "adds" 2 carats, up until 24-carat gold, thus 11 Gold Dust are needed to create a single 24-carat Gold Ingot.

使用方法​

Slimefun Gold Ingots cannot substitute regular Gold Ingots in vanilla recipes. Instead, they are used in new recipes.

Gold Ingot (4-Carat)​

4-carat gold can be turned into Gold Dust by crushing it in an Electric Ingot Pulverizer.

It is also used in the following recipes:

  • Gold Ingot (6-Carat)
  • 方塊放置器

Gold Ingot (6-Carat)​

6-carat gold is used in the following recipes:

  • Gold Ingot (8-Carat)
  • Multimeter
  • Small Backpack

Gold Ingot (8-Carat)​

8-carat gold is used in the following recipes:

  • Gold Ingot (10-Carat)
  • Ancient Altar
  • Ancient Pedestal
  • Common Talisman

Gold Ingot (10-Carat)​

10-carat gold is used in the following recipes:

  • Gold Ingot (12-Carat)
  • Backpack

Gold Ingot (12-Carat)​

12-carat gold is used in the following recipes:

  • Gold Ingot (14-Carat)
  • Gold Armor

Gold Ingot (14-Carat)​

14-carat gold is used in the following recipes:

  • Gold Ingot (16-Carat)
  • Large Backpack

Gold Ingot (16-Carat)​

16-carat gold is used in the following recipes:

  • Gold Ingot (18-Carat)
  • Lava Generator
  • Woven Backpack

Gold Ingot (18-Carat)​

18-carat gold is used in the following recipes:

  • Gold Ingot (20-Carat)
  • Auto-Breeder

Gold Ingot (20-Carat)​

20-carat gold is only used to make 22-carat gold.

Gold Ingot (22-Carat)​

22-carat gold is used in the following recipes:

  • Gold Ingot (24-Carat)
  • Gilded Backpack

Gold Ingot (24-Carat)​

24-carat gold is used in the following recipes:

  • Blistering Ingot (33%)
  • Gilded Iron
  • Gold Block (24-Carat)
  • Radiant Backpack
  • Reinforced Alloy Ingot

Electric Gold Pan

The Electric Gold Pan is an Electric Machine which obtains Sifted Ore from gravel, just like the Gold Pan and the Automated Panning Machine.

Unlike the Electric Dust Washer it still produces by-products and thus the yield rate is exactly the same as its non-electric variants.
